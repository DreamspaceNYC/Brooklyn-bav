Rental Policy:

Thank you for expressing interest in our apartments. We have streamlined our process to enhance clarity and security. Here’s how it works:

  • Virtual Tour & Information: Upon expressing interest, you will receive a link to our website for a virtual tour and apartment details.
  • Rental Questionnaire & Payment Instructions: After reviewing the information, you will receive a link to fill out our rental questionnaire. Please await a confirmation email, which should arrive within five minutes.
  • Payment of : Upon receiving the confirmation email, proceed with the payment. Instructions will be provided separately. Send us a screenshot of the payment confirmation.
  • In-Person Viewing: After we receive your completed questionnaire and the payment, we will send you a receipt and schedule an in-person viewing.
  • Finalizing the Deposit: If you decide to proceed after the in-person viewing, the remaining deposit balance will be requested. If not, you will receive a refund.

We no longer charge an application fee to address concerns about rental scams. This is part of our commitment to a secure and transparent rental process.

Our process is backed by the following legal protections:

  • New York State General Business Law: (Article 22-A, Section 349 & 350) Prohibits deceptive practices in rental transactions, ensuring transparency and fairness.
  • New York State Real Property Law: (Section 231 & 235-e) Requires full disclosure of rental terms and transparency from landlords.
  • NYC Administrative Code: (Title 20, Chapter 1 & 3) Protects consumers from fraudulent practices in housing.
  • NYC Housing Maintenance Code: (Title 27, Section 27-201 & 27-204) Mandates proper maintenance of rental properties and upholds tenant rights.

These laws are designed to protect you from fraudulent activities and ensure a secure rental process.
